Trip to Hautes-Alpes

Jean-Gabriel Ravary

Location

  • Vallee de la Claree: Situated north of Briançon in the Hautes-Alpes department.
  • Vallon des Muandes: Vallon located to the east of the Drayères refuge. The most commonly used valley for crossing to the Vallée Etroite. This valley is also accessible from the Laval refuge.
